Tomato Salad Recipe

Description

This is a quick recipe for a tasty tomato and basil salad. Fast AND healthy!

Ingredients

  • 1 Large wedged Tomato
  • 1 tbs of extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 tbs of basil
  • 1 1/2 tsp of red wine vinegar
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. combine all ingredients into a bowl and toss until thoroughly coated.

  2. Enjoy!
