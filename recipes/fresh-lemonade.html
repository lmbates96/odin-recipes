Fresh Lemonade

Description

Need something to beat that summer heat? This fresh lemonade will certainly get the job done! Easy to make and easy to drink, so its time to get yourself a glass! Makes 1 1/2 quarts.

Ingredients

  • 5 Cups of water
  • 6 Lemons
  • 1 1/4 Cups of white sugar

Steps

  1. Wash and zest the lemons, then peel the lemons and set to the side. Combine lemon zest and sugar in a bowl, then let sit for at least 2 hours.

  2. Boil the water on high, then turn off the heat and add lemon mixture. Stir until completely dissolved.

  3. pour through a strainer into the zest bowl andand let cool to room temperature.

  4. cut lemons in half and squeeze juice into bowl. Pour into a pitcher and chill thoroughly.
